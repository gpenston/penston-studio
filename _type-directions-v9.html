Gill's lineage, the civic instrument fix, and a spec-font wildcard.

Five new tastes, same full-homepage format. I answers the Gill Sans pull with a free descendant. J replaces Overpass-as-header with its calmer civic cousin. K keeps Overpass but demotes it to body copy, per your note. L takes your IBM System favorite and swaps the spec font to something genuinely unclaimed. N is a bonus — a font literally named for this conversation.

On Futura: true Gill Sans is Monotype-licensed (Gill Sans Nova, reachable via Adobe Fonts if you have Creative Cloud) — Cabin below is the free, open-source answer built explicitly on Gill's and Edward Johnston's London Underground lineage, without the licensing question.
