The idea

The read-later queue is a graveyard. You save things with the best intentions, then never go back. Pour Over fixes the loop by surfacing your most recent saves in a clean editorial email, delivered on your schedule.

What it does
01

Recent bookmarks

Pulls your latest saves from Raindrop’s Read Later collection, with cover images, tags, and source metadata.

02

Smart recommendations

Analyzes your archive tags and surfaces related articles via NewsAPI — optional, but worth enabling.

03

Beautiful emails

Responsive design that feels like a real editorial newsletter. Native dark and light mode support for macOS and iOS Mail.

04

Fully automated

Runs on a configurable schedule via GitHub Actions. Daily, weekly, or specific days — no server, no babysitting.
