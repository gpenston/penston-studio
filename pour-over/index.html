Pour Over for Raindrop
GitHub Action · Free & open source

Your read-later list,
brewed fresh daily.

Connect your Raindrop.io read-later collection and get a beautifully formatted email digest, with optional article recommendations, delivered on your schedule. No dashboard, no app. Just your inbox.

View on GitHub Fork and set up

The idea

The read-later queue is a graveyard. You save things with the best intentions, then never go back. Pour Over fixes the loop by surfacing your most recent saves in a clean editorial email, delivered at a time you choose, formatted the way a newsletter should look.

It runs entirely in your own GitHub account. Fork it, add your Raindrop token and SMTP credentials as repository secrets, and your first digest arrives on schedule. Nothing touches any server you don't control.

What it does

Recent bookmarks
Pulls your latest saves from Raindrop's Read Later collection, with cover images, tags, and source metadata.
Smart recommendations
Analyzes your archive tags and surfaces related articles via NewsAPI — optional, but worth enabling.
Beautiful emails
Responsive design that feels like a real editorial newsletter. Native dark and light mode support for macOS and iOS Mail.
Fully automated
Runs on a configurable schedule via GitHub Actions. Daily, weekly, or specific days — no server, no babysitting.
